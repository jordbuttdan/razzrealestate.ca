About
Jordan Buttarazzi — Real Estate Broker

I've been blessed to be on this planet for 30 years.
In another 30, maybe I'll be doing real estate on Mars.

Until then, I'm loving it down here... working with people like you through one of the most important moments in your life:
Moving.

But let's call it what it is. Moving is a life trajectory change...
And it's stressful.

Whether you're buying your first place, moving into a rental with your partner, or selling your starter home to move your family forward, these decisions carry real weight. They shape where you live, how you live, and what your next chapter might look like.

My job is simple: help you make those decisions with clarity, confidence, and the best information possible. With a few laughs along the way.

So you can make smart moves for what matters most.

— JB
