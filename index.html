About

Hi, I'm Jordan. I've been blessed to be on this planet for 30 years. In another 30, maybe I'll be doing real estate on Mars.

Til then, I'm loving it down here, working with people like you through one of the most important parts of your life: your move.

But let's call it what it is: moving is a life trajectory change... And it's stressful.

Whether you're buying your first place, moving into a rental with your partner, or selling your starter home to move your family forward, these decisions carry real weight. They shape where you live, how you live, and what your next chapters looks like.

My job is simple: help you make those decisions with clarity, confidence, and the best information possible.

So you can make smart moves for what matters most.

— JB
